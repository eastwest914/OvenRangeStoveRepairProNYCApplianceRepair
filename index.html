We service all major brands including GE, Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, Wolf, Viking, Thermador and many more.

How quickly can you come for a repair?

We offer same-day and next-day appointments throughout the week. Our technicians are available Monday through Sunday from 8am to 6pm. We understand that a broken cooking appliance is an urgent situation, and we do our best to accommodate your schedule as quickly as possible.

Do you charge a diagnostic fee?

We charge a service call fee which covers the visit and diagnosis. This fee is typically applied toward the total cost of the repair if you choose to proceed. We always provide a clear, upfront quote before beginning any repair work — no surprises on your final bill.

Do you service all NYC boroughs?

Yes, we serve customers across all five boroughs of New York City — Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx and Staten Island. Our Midtown Manhattan location at 347 5th Ave puts us centrally positioned to reach customers throughout the NYC metro area quickly and efficiently.

Is it worth repairing my oven or should I replace it?

In most cases, repair is significantly more cost-effective than replacement, especially for appliances less than 10-15 years old. Our technicians will give you an honest assessment — if the repair cost approaches the value of the appliance, we will tell you so. We never recommend repairs that don't make financial sense for our customers.
